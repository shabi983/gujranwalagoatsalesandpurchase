Policies

Health Guarantee

All goats are inspected and provided with health records. Buyer is encouraged to perform their own inspection at pickup.

Payment

Payments accepted as agreed (cash, bank transfer, or mobile payment). Full payment policy to be arranged at time of sale.

Pickup & Delivery

Local pickup from Kashmir Colony Rahwali. Delivery available upon request for an extra fee — discuss with the owner.

Refunds & Returns

Refunds considered only for undisclosed severe illness present at time of sale and verified by a qualified vet within 48 hours.
